Introduction

Introducing some of the main stuff

The instructors for our course are all from BSD Code and Design Academy. BSD stands for Below Skin Deep, as in everything they teach is detailed and deep. I am very grateful for all the help the instructors have given us, and I have to say I would have got absolutely nothing done if it weren't for them, so I'm very glad that they're here.

Island School Quest Week

I attented this coding course as part of our school's Quest Week. It is a week where normal school lessons are suspended and we students get to choose to take a specialist course in Hong Kong or overseas.

I really like Quest Week because it is a time when we can experience a new variety of 'classes'. The choices are also really good as I think everybody will be able to choose something of interest and suitable challenge.

So far

I have really enjoyed the course so far, as it has been both challenging and rewarding. It is really hard and frustrating at times, but once I have achieved something, I feel as if its a big milestone and I'm proud of myself for overcoming that challenge. After only two days, I know that I'll really enjoy coding for the rest of my life. Not only is it fun, but also a pretty useful skill to have. I can't wait to continue the course, and I'm really looking forward to tomorrow.
